Description

For Project 7, we were tasked with creating a game based on our own life and experiences. The game needed 3 parts or "scenes", and a controllable object that interacts with other objects on the screen

Design Process

Every week day of my life this semester is pretty similar in how it begins. I pack my bag with the stuff I need, head to the train, and commute into the city for class. I figure this process would be the autobiographical aspect of my game, plus it was already 3 different parts, so I could make 3 scenes out of it. For the first "scene" where I would pack my bag, the idea was that several objects would float around the screen, like a laptop, wallet, mask, general items that would be brought to school for the day. The player had to use a mouse "cursor" controlled by the arrow keys to select the different items to pack. After all the items are "packed," the next scene begins where I ride to the train station on my scooter avoiding obstacles along the way. Obstacles like potholes, cars, and trees. After some time passes, the next "scene" would be finding a seat on the train once you make it to the station.
